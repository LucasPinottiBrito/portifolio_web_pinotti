# Product Context: Lucas Pinotti Portfolio

## Users
- Hiring managers and recruiters looking for full-stack engineers with strong backend and automation depth.
- CTOs/Tech Leads seeking expertise in AI agents and operational platforms.
- Professional peers in the software engineering community.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Lucas Pinotti's professional journey as a high-level Full-Stack Software Engineer, moving away from a student persona to a mature technical expert focused on web platforms, backend systems, AI agents, data platforms, and enterprise automation.

## Brand Tone
- **Professional & Technical**: Authoritative but accessible. No fluff.
- **Mature**: Focused on business impact and complex systems.
- **Clean & Minimalist**: Clarity over decoration.
- **Enterprise-ready**: Reliable and robust.

## Register
Brand (Portfolio)

## Strategic Principles
1. **Technical Depth First**: Lead with system complexity and architecture, not just "coding."
2. **Impact Driven**: Highlight problems solved and solutions provided.
3. **Bilingual Seamlessness**: Native-level feel in both English and Portuguese.
4. **Sanitized Transparency**: Show professional EDP projects while strictly adhering to confidentiality.

## Anti-References
- Childish or "cute" visuals.
- Excessive emojis or "developer memes."
- Generic student templates with basic "I like coding" bios.
- Low-contrast, over-decorated landing pages.
- SaaS-cliché "hero-metric" sections without substance.
